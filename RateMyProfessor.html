Developing a web app for UT students to easily find and share professors' ratings

Team: Jiwon Park, Yiwei Wu(me), Benyin Luo, Ray Wei

Programming Languages: JavaScript (with React and Node.js), CSS

Tools Used: Figma & Figjam.

Timeline: Aug 2023 - Nov 2023.

My role: Developed and designed the homepage and user login/registration flow.

Background

Our web app, 'Rate My UT Professor,' is designed to be a resource for University of Texas (UT) students to find ratings for various professors from previous years. It provides a more straightforward alternative to searching through Reddit or other sources. Additionally, this web app enables UT students to securely log in with their UT EID and leave reviews for their professors they took classes with.
