Examing A11Y Issues in digital maps through a different lens

Tools Used: Figma & Google Slide.

Timeline: Oct. 2022(4 weeks)

My role: UX Researcher

Background

This is an assignment for the Accessible UX course at UT Austin. In this project, I represented as a UX Researcher and collaborated with a classmate who represented as a UX Designer to conduct the evaluation of an A11y issue in digital mapping areas and provide recommendations for future map design.


Feel Free to go through our report slides to see the findings and recommendations! ⬇️
