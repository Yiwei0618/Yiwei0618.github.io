Hi, I'm Yiwei!

I am a master's student at the University of Texas at Austin, studying Information Science with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction.

Previously, I studied Public Health and Data Science at the University of Washington, Seattle.

Coming from an interdisciplinary background, I have a strong interest in bringing technology into the community to create positive social impacts. Aiming to address critical HCI issues, my research focuses on discovering social movements in online settings by applying a theory-guided approach to understand the impact of technology on social interaction and individual well-being.
